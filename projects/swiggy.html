Tableau Marketing Analytics Case Study

Swiggy Marketing Analytics Dashboard

Insights for Customer Growth, Engagement & Campaign Strategy

This project analyzes Swiggy marketing performance to help the business understand customer behavior, campaign effectiveness, order patterns, and growth opportunities. The dashboard and story are designed for marketing stakeholders who need clear, actionable insights for decision-making.

View Tableau Story Open Tableau Workbook

Business Context

Swiggy operates in a highly competitive food delivery market where customer retention, campaign targeting, order frequency, and user engagement directly influence business growth. This analysis focuses on turning marketing and customer behavior data into practical insights that can support campaign planning and improve customer experience.

Business Objective

  • Understand customer behavior and ordering patterns across key business segments.
  • Identify marketing opportunities that can improve engagement and retention.
  • Evaluate performance patterns that help the marketing team target customers more effectively.
  • Build a Tableau dashboard and story that communicate insights clearly for business stakeholders.

Analysis Approach

Data Exploration

Reviewed customer, order, campaign, and behavior-related fields to understand business patterns.

Segmentation

Analyzed customer groups and behavior patterns to identify high-value and improvement segments.

Dashboarding

Created an interactive Tableau dashboard for filtering, comparison, and stakeholder exploration.

Storytelling

Built a Tableau story to present insights in a structured, business-friendly flow.

Key Insights

Customer Behavior Patterns

The analysis highlights differences in customer activity, ordering behavior, and engagement patterns that can support better campaign targeting.

Marketing Opportunity Areas

Customer segments with lower engagement or inconsistent ordering can be targeted with personalized campaigns, offers, and retention strategies.

Stakeholder-Friendly Reporting

The Tableau story turns dashboard findings into a clear narrative that helps marketing teams move from analysis to action.

Recommendations

Improve Targeting

  • Use customer behavior segments to design more personalized marketing campaigns.
  • Prioritize campaigns for users with high potential but lower recent engagement.

Strengthen Retention

  • Identify customers showing reduced activity and target them with win-back campaigns.
  • Track repeat order behavior to improve long-term customer value.

Support Marketing Decisions

  • Use the dashboard as a recurring reporting tool for campaign review.
  • Combine dashboard insights with campaign experiments to validate business impact.
